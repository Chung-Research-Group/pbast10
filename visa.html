Visa Information & Invitation Letter

PBAST10 welcomes participants from around the world. We are happy to assist with your entry requirements to the Republic of Korea.

Entry Requirements to South Korea

K-ETA — Electronic Travel Authorization

Required for citizens of visa-waiver countries (most of Europe, North America, Australia, etc.).

Apply onlinek-eta.go.kr →
Fee~₩10,000 (USD 7–8)
Processing24–72 hours (apply ≥72h before departure)
Validity3 years (or until passport expires)

C-3 Visa — Short-Term Visit

Required for nationalities that are not visa-exempt. Apply at the Korean Embassy or Consulate in your country.

Processing times vary by country — apply well in advance and check requirements with your local Korean mission.

Official Invitation Letter

The PBAST10 Organizing Committee will issue an official invitation letter to support your K-ETA or visa application. (Request procedure to be finalized.)

  1. Complete your conference registration and payment
  2. Fill out the Invitation Letter Request Form (link TBD)
  3. Provide: full name (as on passport), passport number & nationality, date of birth, affiliation, position, mailing address, dates of stay, and presentation title (if applicable)
  4. The letter is emailed as a PDF within 5–7 business days

A hard copy can be provided upon special request (additional postage fee may apply).

Important Notes

  • The invitation letter is for visa/K-ETA application purposes only — it does not guarantee approval or provide financial support
  • Visa and K-ETA decisions rest solely with Korean immigration authorities
  • Apply for your K-ETA or visa well in advance
  • Your passport must be valid at least 6 months from the date of entry into Korea
