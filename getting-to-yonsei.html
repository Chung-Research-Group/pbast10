Venue & Accommodation

Getting to Yonsei University

Sinchon Campus · From Incheon International Airport (ICN) to the conference venue.

Option 1 · Most recommended

Airport Limousine Bus 6011

Direct, comfortable seats, luggage space, and English announcements — the best choice for most international participants.

Fare₩17,000 (adult)
Travel time60–75 min
FrequencyEvery 20–30 min
  1. Go to the Arrival Floor (1F) after baggage claim
  2. Buy a ticket at an airport limousine ticket counter
  3. Board Bus No. 6011 (Sinchon / Yonsei route)
  4. Get off at Yonsei University main gate
  5. Walk or take a short taxi, depending on the final conference building
Option 2 · Cheapest

AREX Train + Subway

Cheap and reliable, unaffected by traffic.

Total cost~₩5,000–6,000
Total time70–80 min
  1. Follow signs to AREX (Airport Railroad) on B1
  2. Take the All-Stop Train (not Express) to Hongik University Station (~50–55 min)
  3. Transfer to Subway Line 2 → one stop to Sinchon Station
  4. Use Exit 2 (best for Yonsei) or Exit 3
  5. Walk 8–12 minutes or take a short taxi (~₩3,000)
Option 3 · Most convenient

Taxi

Door-to-door and often the most practical option when regular public transit is unavailable.

Regular taxi~₩70,000–90,000
Deluxe taxi~₩100,000–130,000
Travel time45–70 min
  • Use the official taxi stand outside the terminal
  • International (English-speaking) taxis: yellow/orange signs
  • Show the driver: "연세대학교 신촌캠퍼스"
  • Kakao T app works great — download in advance

Essential Practical Tips

  • T-money Card — buy at airport convenience stores; works for bus, subway, and taxi
  • Navigation — KakaoMap or Naver Map (English available); Google Maps is less accurate for buses
  • Wi-Fi — free at the airport and on most buses/trains
  • Cards — most places accept international cards; ATMs everywhere
  • Late-night arrivals — check the current last-departure times; take an official taxi if regular transit has ended
