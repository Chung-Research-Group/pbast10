Scientific Scope

PBAST10 welcomes contributions related to, but not limited to, the following topics:

01
Adsorption equilibria and kinetics
02
Gas storage and separation
03
Carbon capture, utilization, and storage
04
Water treatment and environmental adsorption
05
Porous materials: MOFs, zeolites, carbons, polymers
06
Diffusion and transport in confined spaces
07
Molecular simulation and machine learning
08
Thermodynamics and interfacial phenomena
09
Industrial applications and process development
