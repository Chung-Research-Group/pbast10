Publication Opportunities

PBAST10 Special Issues

PBAST10 is coordinating publication opportunities with peer-reviewed journals in adsorption science and related fields. Participating journals will be announced as individual arrangements are confirmed.

Participating Journals

To be announced

Discussions are ongoing with relevant journals. This page will remain open to multiple participating journals, and confirmed opportunities will be listed here individually.

Information for each special issue

When a journal is confirmed, its announcement will include:

  • Journal title and special-issue scope
  • Eligible article types and submission deadline
  • Guest Editors and submission link
  • Article processing charges, discounts, or waivers, if applicable

Editorial independence

Submission to a participating special issue will be separate from conference abstract submission and conference registration.

All manuscripts will be subject to the journal's standard editorial screening and independent peer-review process. Acceptance of a PBAST10 abstract or presentation does not guarantee publication.

Journal inquiries

Editors or publishers interested in discussing a PBAST10 special issue may contact the Organizing Committee.
