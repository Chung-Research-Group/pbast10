Welcome

PBAST10 brings together researchers, engineers, and industry professionals working at the frontiers of adsorption science, porous materials, molecular transport, and separation technology.

The conference provides a forum for sharing recent advances, exchanging ideas, and building new collaborations across the Pacific Basin and beyond.
